5. Risk Assessment - Cost of Inaction

VOLTZITE (PRC-linked) Pre-Positioning

Active reconnaissance of US electric utilities per CISA AA24-038A. The Columbia River Basin produces 40% of US hydroelectric generation; these 4 dams are part of that grid. Current detection capability in this fictional scenario is limited.

CHERNOVITE ICS Attack Framework

PIPEDREAM/INCONTROLLER targets safety controllers across vendors. First cross-industry ICS malware. Without protocol-level monitoring, this type of attack is invisible to IT security tools.

Regulatory Non-Compliance

EP 1130-2-510 requires OT asset inventory and network monitoring. Current state: manual spreadsheets and limited continuous OT monitoring. Audit risk is increasing with federal OT security emphasis.

Cascading Infrastructure Impact

A successful attack on Columbia River dams affects power generation modeled at a 5,970 MW planning basis, navigation, flood control, irrigation, and fish passage across 3 states. The blast radius extends far beyond USACE.
