## Ovation I/O
Emerson’s Ovation control and I/O technology is designed to support long-term process reliability and expandability. Ovation I/O features modular, plug-in components that enhance long-term reliability and simplify maintenance. Fast DIN rail installation, built-in connectors, and electronic ID streamline setup and configuration. With hot-swappable modules, standardized diagnostics, and fewer styles to manage, Ovation I/O reduces inventory costs while delivering robust, scalable performance.​
[Learn More](https://www.emerson.com/is/content/emerson/en/corporate/shared-assets/documents/pws_002864-ovation-io-overview.pdf)

## Enterprise Data Solutions (EDS)
Ovation Enterprise Data Solutions (EDS) securely aggregates real-time and historical data from Ovation and other systems, enabling remote visualization of plant performance across single or multi-site operations. With mobile access, original graphics, and Excel/web service integration, EDS empowers faster, data-driven decisions all while enhancing reliability, reducing costs, and supporting enterprise-wide digital transformation.​
[Learn More](https://www.emerson.com/en/automation-systems/distributed-control-systems-dcs/ovation-distributed-control-system/eds)
