#### See the Catalyst IE3400 Rugged Series difference
Simplify and make your operations ready for the future with a rock-solid networking and security foundation that's built on easy-to-deploy, high-performing, and secure Catalyst IE3400 Rugged Series industrial switches.

### Deliver continuous operations
Keep your network performing at its peak, with all-gigabit fiber ports, Power over Ethernet (PoE)-enabled copper ports, advanced resiliency and industrial protocols, and industry certifications.

### Optimize operations with IT best practices
Simplify operations with enterprise-grade networking features of the Cisco IOS XE operating system and network management with Cisco Catalyst Center.

### Unify security with networking
Eliminate point products and simplify the network architecture by embedding visibility and security as software features. Get details on connected assets with Cisco Cyber Vision. Limit the spread of malware with segmentation based on the Cisco TrustSec solution.

### Simplify and secure remote access
Set up zero-trust network access (ZTNA) with the hosted Cisco Secure Equipment Access gateway, so that your operations team, contractors, and vendors can troubleshoot and maintain OT assets, while you retain full control.

Select the model that's best-suited for your needs. Add expansion modules as your requirements evolve.

### IE-3400-8T2S
Modular design, gigabit copper ports, and fiber uplinks, for industrial DIN rail deployments
- 8 GE copper and 2 GE fiber ports, expandable up to 26 ports
- Advanced industrial security and resiliency protocols
- Cisco IOx application hosting environment
- Cisco SD-Access Policy Extended Node
- Microsegmentation based on Cisco TrustSec technology
- Managed by Cisco Catalyst Center

### IE-3400-8P2S
Modular design, gigabit copper ports with PoE, and fiber uplinks for industrial DIN rail deployments
- 8 GE PoE/PoE+ copper and 2 GE fiber ports, expandable up to 26 ports
- Up to 240W in base and 480W power budget with expansion modules
- Advanced industrial security and resiliency protocols
- Cisco IOx application hosting environment
- Cisco SD-Access Policy Extended Node
- Microsegmentation based on Cisco TrustSec technology
- Managed by Cisco Catalyst Center

### IEM-3400-8S
For adding more fiber ports to your Catalyst IE3400 Rugged Series base switches
- Advanced expansion module for all base models
- 8 GE fiber ports

### IEM-3400-8T
For adding more copper ports to your Catalyst IE3400 Rugged Series base switches
- Advanced expansion module for all base models
- 8 GE copper ports

### IEM-3400-8P
For adding more copper ports with PoE to your Catalyst IE3400 Rugged Series base switches
- Advanced expansion module for IE-3400-8P2S
- 8 GE PoE/PoE+ copper ports, and additional power budget of up to 240W, for a total of up to 480W

### Catalyst IE3300 expansion modules
Catalyst IE3400 Rugged Series switches can also use expansion modules for Cisco Catalyst IE3300 Rugged Series switches, with some limitations.
