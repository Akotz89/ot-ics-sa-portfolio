## Why SIMATIC S7-400?
The flexibly configurable SIMATIC S7-400 controller series is designed for standard and failsafe applications in the manufacturing and process automation industry. The SIMATIC S7-400H and FH systems are ideal for data-intensive tasks. Both S7-400 and the innovated S7-410 product line provide long-term security of investment and will stay the hardware backbone of the PCS 7 control system.

## Featured capabilities

### Fully configurable
The flexibly configurable SIMATIC S7-400 controller series designed by Siemens is suitable for demanding applications in the process industry.

### High security
These controllers use SIMATIC S7-400H and FH systems, which ensure a high degree of security and availability.

### Ongoing development
SIMATIC S7-400 controllers are a long-term investment. You get permanent access to your controller series through a consistent supply of spare parts and availability of components.
