## Precisely Monitor and Control Operations with Powerful Enterprise SCADA
- Secure-by-design data collection from anywhere to monitor, control, and analyze your processes
- High performance HMI with native and HTML5-based visualization for advanced dashboards
- Advanced alarming and notifications with customizable event management
- Extensibility via Python, .NET, and VB scripting, plus APIs for optimized configuration
- Enterprise support, including cloud and hybrid deployment options
- Historization to SQL and Proficy Historian with dynamic graphical replay (DGR)
- High Availability support with redundancy and 24/7 uptime
- Reporting tools for KPIs, dashboards, and compliance data
