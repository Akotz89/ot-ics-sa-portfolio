Source Priority Order

1. Official government publications (CISA, NIST, USACE, NVD, Federal Register)
2. Vendor-published documentation (Dragos, GE Vernova, Rockwell, Siemens, Emerson)
3. Federal procurement databases (USAspending.gov, GSA Advantage, SEWP)
4. Press coverage with named sources (intelligencecommunitynews.com, executivebiz.com)
5. Web captures preserved as PDF when original source may change or require authentication

Verification Standard

Each claim was checked against the primary source document. Where public sources showed inconsistent data (e.g., USACE dam capacity figures varying across fact sheets and narrative pages), the portfolio uses a conservative planning basis and notes the variance. Five corrections were applied during the red-team audit on May 10, 2026.
